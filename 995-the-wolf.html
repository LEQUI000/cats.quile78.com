​Great night for #WolfFridayNightLights! See who our featured Football team and band is!

​Posted on September 15, 2017
​Brought to you by Dunkin Donuts, it’s #WolfFridayNightLights !

Our featured “Best Band in the Land” this week is the Sachse High School Mustang Band.

Under the direction of Holly Taylor, Kevin MacDonald, John Robinson, Taylor Tucker, and Damien Waites, the Mustang Band has been competitive in many local Marching Invitationals as well as the Area C UIL Marching Finals for numerous years.  The Mustang Band takes pride in their unique show concepts and are looking forward to competing with their 2017 Contest show “Curiosity Killed the Cat” on Saturday, October 7,  2017, at Classic on the Lake in Little Elm. They extend a huge thank-you to their phenomenal Booster Program for all of their hard work and support as well as the Sachse High School Administration.  Come out and see the Mustang band perform at any Mustang Football game and follow us on Twitter @SachseBand.  Run Mustangs Run!
